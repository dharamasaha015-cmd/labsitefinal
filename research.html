About Our Lab

The Advanced Research Laboratory is dedicated to cutting-edge interdisciplinary research in materials science, condensed matter physics, and energy devices. Our mission is to develop innovative solutions for sustainable technologies and to train the next generation of researchers.

What We Do

We work on diverse research themes, combining experiment and theory:

  • Thin Films and Nanostructures for optoelectronics
  • Solar Cells and Advanced Photovoltaics
  • Energy Storage Materials and Solid-State Batteries
  • In-situ Microscopy and Characterization Tools
  • Machine Learning for Materials Discovery
