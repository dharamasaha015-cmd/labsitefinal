Our Laboratory Facilities

State-of-the-art instruments empowering cutting-edge research

Available Equipment

X-Ray Diffractometer (XRD)

Used for determining crystal structure, phase identification, and quantitative analysis of materials.

Scanning Electron Microscope (SEM)

Provides high-resolution surface morphology imaging and elemental composition analysis with EDX.

UV-Vis Spectrophotometer

Measures optical absorbance, transmittance, and band gap of nanomaterials and thin films.

FTIR Spectrometer

Analyzes functional groups and chemical bonding in organic and inorganic materials.

Electrochemical Workstation

Used for cyclic voltammetry, impedance spectroscopy, and studying energy storage devices.

Thin Film Coater

Used for preparing uniform thin films and coatings for device fabrication and testing.
